Podcast ROI is not just downloads. For business owners, the real return comes from authority, trust, and client acquisition.

## What is podcast ROI?

**Quick Answer:** Podcast ROI measures the return generated from your podcast in terms of leads, clients, brand authority, and content output-not just audience size.

## What actually drives ROI?

- trust and credibility  
- content volume  
- distribution reach  
- inbound leads  

## ROI vs vanity metrics

| Metric | Type |
|---|---|
| Downloads | Vanity |
| Leads | Real |
| Clients | Real |
| Engagement | Mixed |

## The AJ Digital ROI Model

1. Content output  
2. Authority growth  
3. Lead generation  
4. Conversion
