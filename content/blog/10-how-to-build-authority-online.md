Authority online is built through consistency, positioning, and systems-not viral moments. The goal is to become a trusted voice in your niche.

## What is authority online?

**Quick Answer:** Authority online is the perception that you are a trusted, credible source in your field, built through consistent content and clear positioning.

## What builds authority?

- consistent content  
- clear messaging  
- strong positioning  
- distribution systems  
- time  

## Biggest mistake
