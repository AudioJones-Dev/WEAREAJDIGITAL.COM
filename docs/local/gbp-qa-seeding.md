# GBP Q&A Seeding System

## 1. Q&A OBJECTIVE
The Q&A section of a Google Business Profile is heavily underutilized. Anybody can ask a question, and anybody can answer it. To control the narrative and optimize the profile for AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and Local SEO, you should proactively **ask and answer your own questions**. 

**Objectives:**
- **Reduce Friction:** Answer the exact logistical questions that block clients from converting to a sales call.
- **Answer Local-Intent Questions:** Seed "Miami" and "South Florida" terms naturally via direct questions.
- **Reinforce Service Authority:** Ensure the answers read like authoritative, precise definitions that align with your Next.js service pages.

---

## 2. ANSWER WRITING RULES
- **Direct & Answer-First:** Start the response with a clear "Yes," "No," or the exact data requested. 
- **Concise:** Keep responses under 3-4 sentences.
- **Localized:** Naturally weave in local neighborhood/city markers when applicable.
- **Service-Aligned:** Use the exact phrasing of your core services ("Content Systems," "Podcast Production," "AI Consulting").

---
