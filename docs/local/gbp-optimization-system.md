# Google Business Profile Optimization System

## 1. GBP STRATEGY OVERVIEW

### The Role of GBP in AJ Digital Acquisition
For AJ Digital, the Google Business Profile (GBP) is not just a digital yellow pages listing; it is the **primary trust engine and conversion layer** for local South Florida search traffic. When high-ticket founders search for "podcast production Miami" or "AI consulting," the GBP Map Pack is often their first touchpoint. A highly optimized, active profile signals immediate authority, operational competence, and geographic relevance, converting raw local search volume directly into `/apply` strategy calls.

### Relation to Local Pages
This GBP profile acts as the top-of-funnel validation layer that feeds directly into AJ Digital's localized website pages. The geographic signals generated by the profile (service areas, local posts, localized Q&A) algorithmically support the ranking of our dedicated Next.js local pages (`/podcast-production-miami`, `/ai-consulting-miami`, `/content-systems-miami`). 

### Relation to Reviews, Photos, and Posts
An empty profile looks like a dead business. AJ Digital’s profile must visually and textually demonstrate an active system:
- **Reviews** provide peer validation from fellow founders.
- **Photos** prove we have legitimate, high-end infrastructure (not just a hobbyist in a bedroom).
- **Posts** show that we are actively deploying strategy, providing fresh AEO-extracted educational content directly on the SERP.

## 8. GBP MAINTENANCE SYSTEM (OPERATIONS CHECKLIST)

Google frequently updates profiles based on third-party data or user suggestions. Strict maintenance is required.

### Launch Checklist (One-Time):
- [ ] Claim and verify profile.
- [ ] Input all baseline data, categories, and UTM links.
- [ ] Upload initial batch of 15+ high-quality photos.
- [ ] Add all 3 core services with detailed descriptions.
- [ ] Seed the 15 foundational Q&As.
- [ ] Publish the first "Welcome/Who We Are" Post.

### Weekly Checklist:
- [ ] **Publish 1-2 new GBP Posts** (See Posting Calendar).
- [ ] **Check for Google Updates:** Review the dashboard for any orange text indicating Google altered your hours, services, or description. Accept or discard.
- [ ] **Respond to Reviews:** Reply to any new reviews within 24 hours.

### Monthly Checklist:
- [ ] **Upload 3-5 new photos:** Keep the visual profile fresh (new studio shots, BTS, team photos).
- [ ] **Review Insights/Analytics:** Check search terms, map views, and website clicks to ensure local traction is growing.
- [ ] **Review Solicitation:** Send out 2-3 review request templates to recently satisfied clients.

### Quarterly Checklist:
- [ ] **Audit Service Offerings:** Ensure GBP services match any new deployments on `weareajdigital.com`.
- [ ] **Update Q&A:** Add new questions based on actual sales calls over the last 90 days.
- [ ] **Refresh Cover Photo:** Rotate the primary cover image to test conversion rates.
