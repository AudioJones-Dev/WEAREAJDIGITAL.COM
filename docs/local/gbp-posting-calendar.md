# GBP Posting Calendar & Strategy

## 1. POSTING STRATEGY

### Objective of GBP Posts
Google Business Profile posts are not Facebook or Instagram updates; their primary objective is to act as **micro-landing pages directly on the search engine results page (SERP)**. They signal an active, operating business to Google's algorithm, capture high-intent "near me" searchers, and drive direct clicks to our service pages and the `/apply` funnel. 

### Ideal Cadence
- **Frequency:** 1 to 2 times per week. 
- **Lifespan:** Native GBP "Update" posts previously expired after 7 days, but now remain in the profile's history. Consistent weekly posting ensures the "Update" carousel always looks fresh to new visitors.

### Content Pillars (Tied to AJ Digital Services)
1. **Podcast Production Focus** (Studio quality, on-site Miami shoots, strategy).
2. **AI Consulting Focus** (Automation tips, workflow optimizations, time saved).
3. **Content Systems Focus** (Repurposing math, 1-to-30 asset leverage).
4. **Local Miami/South Florida Excellence** (Highlighting local clients, local landmarks, or in-person strategy sessions).
5. **Results & Case Studies** (Social proof, transformation metrics).

---

## 2. POST WRITING RULES

- **No fluff:** Get straight to the point. The preview text only shows the first 100 characters.
- **Answer-first:** Start with the core value proposition or solution immediately.
- **Action-oriented:** Always direct the reader to take a logical next step.
- **Compliant:** Do not use prohibited content (excessive capitalization, gimmicky spam, URLs in the post body—use the dedicated button instead).
- **Format:** Short, punchy lines. Use 1–2 emojis for visual breaks.

## 3. POST TEMPLATE SYSTEM

When generating new posts, use these core structural templates:

### Template A: The Service Spotlight
**Headline:** Did you know we handle [Specific Service] in [Location]?
**Body:** Stop struggling with [Pain Point]. Our [Service Name] system takes the heavy lifting off your plate. We handle [Feature 1], [Feature 2], and [Feature 3] so you can focus on running your business.
**Button:** Learn More → Links to localized service page.

### Template B: The Educational Tip / AEO Snippet
**Headline:** How to solve [Common Problem] using [AJ Digital Method].
**Body:** Most founders waste time doing [Inefficient Action]. Instead, try this 3-step system: 1. [Step 1] 2. [Step 2] 3. [Step 3]. We build these exact systems for our clients every day.
**Button:** Book / Apply → Links to `/apply`.

### Template C: Local Relevance / Behind the Scenes
**Headline:** On-site with a great client in [Miami Neighborhood / City].
**Body:** Great shoot today in [Location] helping [Client Type] build their podcast engine. There's nothing better than helping South Florida entrepreneurs scale their authority. If your business needs a professional content system, let's talk.
**Button:** Learn More → Links to `/podcast-production-miami/`.
