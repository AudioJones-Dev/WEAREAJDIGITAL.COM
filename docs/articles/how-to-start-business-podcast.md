## H2: What is a business podcast?

A business podcast is a content platform designed to build authority, attract an audience, and generate leads by sharing insights, interviews, and expertise in a specific niche. Unlike entertainment podcasts, business podcasts are structured around strategy and outcomes.

---

## H2: Why should you start a business podcast in 2026?

Businesses are starting podcasts because they provide long-form content that builds trust and can be repurposed into multiple formats across platforms.

### Key benefits:

- Build authority in your niche  
- Create consistent content  
- Generate inbound leads  
- Repurpose into blogs, clips, and posts  
- Establish thought leadership  

---

## H2: What do you need to start a business podcast?

Starting a podcast requires a combination of basic equipment, software, and a clear content strategy.

### Basic setup:

- Microphone (USB or XLR)  
- Recording software (Riverside, Descript)  
- Editing tools  
- Podcast hosting platform  
- Content plan

## H2: Common mistakes when starting a podcast

Most podcasts fail due to lack of structure and consistency.

### Mistakes to avoid:

- No clear niche or audience  
- Inconsistent publishing  
- Poor audio quality  
- No content repurposing  
- No distribution strategy  

👉 Most of these are solved with a system—not more effort.

Most business podcasts begin seeing measurable growth within 60–90 days when consistent publishing and distribution systems are in place.
