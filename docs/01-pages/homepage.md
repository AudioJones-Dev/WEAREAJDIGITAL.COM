# Homepage Strategy

## Purpose
The main landing experience designed to immediately convey value, agitate the core problem, provide the solution, and drive users to the main conversion funnel.

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero
- Needs a strong, value-driven H1.
- Clear subheadline setting expectations.
- Primary CTA button driving to `/apply`.

### 2. Pain
- Identify the explicit problems the target audience faces.
- Validate their current struggles.

### 3. Solution
- Introduce our unique mechanism/solution.
- Pivot from problem to opportunity.

### 4. Services
- High-level cards interlinking to specific `/services/[slug]` pages.
- Highlight Podcast Production, AI Consulting, etc.

### 5. Proof
- Testimonials, case studies, or logos of past clients.
- Hard data on results.

### 6. Process
- 3-step breakdown of how engagement works.
- Keep it simple and actionable.

### 7. CTA
- Final page block with a compelling offer.
- Button routing to the apply flow.
