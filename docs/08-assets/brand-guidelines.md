# Brand Guidelines

## Purpose
Maintains visual and tonal consistency across all digital interfaces for AJ Digital.

## Typography
- **Headings (H1-H3):** Primary display font, weights, letter-spacing.
- **Body Text:** highly legible sans-serif, optimal line height (e.g., 1.6).

## Color Palette
- **Primary Brand Color:** Hex/RGB definition.
- **Secondary/Accent Colors:** Used for buttons, highlights.
- **Neutrals:** Slate/gray shades for borders, muted text.
- Must ensure WCAG AA accessibility compliance for contrast.

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Professional but accessible:** Speak directly to the client's problem.
- **Authority-driven:** Position as the expert guiding the hero (the client).
- **Concise:** Avoid jargon; focus on outcomes and action.
