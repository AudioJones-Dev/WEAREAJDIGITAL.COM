## Enforcement Rules

- Pillars should link to every directly relevant cluster, service page, local page, flagship proof page, and `/apply`.
- Clusters should link to the parent pillar, at least two sibling clusters, one to two services, relevant local pages when geography fits, the flagship proof page, and `/apply`.
- Service pages should link into their pillar, cluster support content, flagship proof, and `/apply`.
- Local pages should link to the parent service, the strongest pillar, and `/apply` early and again mid-page.
- The case study should keep routing users into all three services, at least one pillar, and `/apply`.
