# Case Study Internal Linking Rules

Case studies are the ultimate proof layer of the AJ Digital website. They must heavily interlink with our educational content (blog) and commercial pages (services).

## Core Linking Rules

Every case study **must** contain contextual text links back to the systems it utilized. 

### Required Outbound Links (from Case Study to Commercial/Funnel Pages)
Depending on what the case study is about, it MUST link to at least one of these:
* `/services/podcast-production/`
* `/services/ai-consulting/`
* `/services/content-systems/`
* **Always Link To:** `/apply` (Mandatory CTA at the end of every case study).

### Bi-Directional Linking with the Blog (Authority Loop)
* **Blog → Case Study**: When a blog post teaches a concept (e.g., "How to automate social media with AI"), it MUST link to a relevant case study as proof ("See how we built this exact AI workflow for a real estate client").
* **Case Study → Blog**: When a case study mentions a technical execution detail, it should link back to a pillar or cluster article that explains the *how* in depth.

### Bi-Directional Linking with Services
* **Service Page → Case Study**: Every service page should embed or link to the 2-3 most relevant case studies to prove capability. The `/services/podcast-production/` page must showcase Podcast case studies.

## Link Formatting for AEO
* Anchor text must be descriptive and natural. Do not use "click here."
* Use exact match or closely related keywords for anchor text pointing to service pages (e.g., "...we deployed our *content repurposing system* to...").
