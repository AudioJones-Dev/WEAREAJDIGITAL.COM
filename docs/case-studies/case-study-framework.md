# AJ Digital Case Study Framework™

This framework dictates how we capture, structure, and position client success stories. Case studies are not just past work; they are future sales assets.

## The 6-Step Framework

### 1. Identify Starting Point (Baseline)
Before implementing any system, document the client's current reality. What is their output volume? How much time are they wasting? What is their current inbound lead flow? We need a clear "Before" to contrast with the "After."

### 2. Define Bottlenecks
Pinpoint the exact friction stopping their growth. Is it a lack of video editing skills? Inconsistent posting? Absence of an AI-driven workflow bridging their podcast to their newsletter? 

### 3. Implement System
Detail the specific AJ Digital solution deployed. Whether it's our Podcast Production infrastructure, a bespoke Content Repurposing System, or specialized AI Consulting workflows. 

### 4. Execute Consistently
Show the system in action. Document the timeline, the deliverables, and the mechanics of the operation. Prove that the system works in the real world, not just on paper.

### 5. Measure Outputs
Quantify the impact. This is the most crucial step for conversion. We must capture:
* **Time Saved** (Operational efficiency)
* **Content Generated** (Volume and scale)
* **Leads / Revenue** (Business impact)

### 6. Optimize and Scale
Case studies should show a trajectory. What did we learn after the first 30 days? How did we tweak the AI prompts? How did we improve the podcast hook retention? Show the continuous improvement layer.
