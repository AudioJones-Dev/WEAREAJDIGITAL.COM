# Keyword Strategy

## Purpose
Maps the primary, secondary, and long-tail keywords that power the SEO and content engines.

## Core Pillars
- Terms related to high-level offerings (e.g., AI agency, content automation).

## Secondary Keywords
- Service specific (e.g., podcast production services for businesses).

## AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
- Questions to answer explicitly on pages for ChatGPT/Perplexity optimization.
- "What is...", "How much does...", "Why should I..."

## Monitoring
- Tools used for tracking (Ahrefs, Semrush, Search Console).
- KPIs for organic success.
