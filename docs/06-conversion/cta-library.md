# CTA Library

## Purpose
A standardized collection of Call To Actions used across the site to ensure brand consistency and optimize conversion rates.

## Primary CTA
- **Text:** "Apply to Work With Us" / "Book a Strategy Call"
- **Style:** High contrast, solid background, highly visible.
- **Link:** `/apply`

## Secondary CTA
- **Text:** "See Case Studies" / "Read the Blog"
- **Style:** Outline or ghost button.
- **Link:** Relevant cluster or proof page.

## Soft CTA (In-content)
- **Text:** "Want to automate this? Let's talk."
- **Style:** Inline text link or subtle section banner.
- **Link:** Relevant service page.
