## H2: How does our AI consulting process work?

Our AI consulting process works by diagnosing your immediate pain points before prescribing complex technology. We never suggest software for the sake of software; every deployment is reverse-engineered strictly from a desired revenue or efficiency outcome.

### The AJ Digital AI Implementation Framework™

1. **Audit bottlenecks**: We rigorously analyze your current digital workflows to isolate the specific tasks costing your company time.
2. **Identify high-leverage use cases**: We pinpoint exactly where AI will provide the fastest, highest ROI.
3. **Select the right tools**: We curate the specific tech stack (ChatGPT, Claude, n8n) required, avoiding bloated software.
4. **Design simple workflows**: We map the logic paths to ensure robust, failure-resistant automation.
5. **Implement and test**: We build the architecture, connect the APIs, and rigorously test the outputs.
6. **Train and optimize**: We hand over standard operating procedures and train your team to manage the system confidently.

## H2: Frequently Asked Questions

### H3: What is AI consulting?
AI consulting is a specialized advisory service where experts help a business identify operational constraints and deploy targeted artificial intelligence technologies to automate workflows, accelerate production, and reduce overhead.

### H3: Who needs an AI consultant?
An AI consultant is necessary for entrepreneurs or operational teams who are overwhelmed by administrative tasks and understand the leverage of AI, but require technical expertise to actually string the architectures together.

### H3: How much does AI consulting cost?
AI consulting rates vary based on implementation complexity. A strategic roadmap or lightweight operational automation build might start at $2,000, while designing vast, deeply integrated company knowledge bases can exceed $10,000+.

### H3: What tools do you recommend?
Tool recommendations are entirely dependent on your business logic. We frequently implement stacks that rely on OpenAI APIs, Claude, Descript for audio engineering, and n8n to choreograph complex webhook routing.

### H3: Can you help implement workflows?
Yes. Implementation is our primary differentiator. We do not just give you advice; we write the logic, integrate the software, establish the quality guardrails, and train your team on exactly how to use the workflows.

### H3: Is AI consulting only for large companies?
No, AI consulting is highly relevant for small businesses and nimble entrepreneurs. Focused AI deployment can effectively simulate the output of three administrative hires, allowing small teams to operate at enterprise scale.
