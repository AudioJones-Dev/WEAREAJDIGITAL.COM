# Homepage Structure (`/`)

**Target:** Root URL
**Goal:** Serve as the definitive Front Door connecting all Services, Proof, Education, Local SEO grids, and Apply Funnels in a monolithic high-conversion architecture.

# Architecture Breakdown

## 1. Hero
* Bold positioning: "Podcast, AI, and Content Systems That Turn Attention Into Clients"
* Directly routes high-intent traffic into the `/apply` qualifying form.

## 2. Dynamic Trust Matrix
* Immediate E-E-A-T builder reinforcing output metrics (30+ Assets) before user scrolls.

## 3. Product Hub (What AJ Digital Builds)
Categorical mapping preventing user confusion, routing out to the 3 main commercial branches:
* `/services/podcast-production/`
* `/services/ai-consulting/`
* `/services/content-systems/`

## 4. ICP Alignment (Who This Is For)
* Identifies Entrepreneurs, Coaches, Brands, and Orgs. Pain vs. Fit mapping drives psychological resonance.

## 5. Differentiator Table
* "Typical Agency vs. AJ Digital" positioning. Converts visitors searching for "editors" into clients valuing "systems."

## 6. Process Definition 
* "The AJ Digital Authority Engine™" 6-step breakdown, eliminating operational ambiguity.

## 7. The Core Proof
* Deep embeds the primary Case Study (`/case-studies/podcast-authority-system`) pushing a strong narrative of exactly what a "system" output metric looks like natively.

## 8. South Florida SEO Anchor
* Injects Local SEO strength by bridging the national brand to the localized `/podcast-production-miami/`, `/ai-consulting-miami/`, and `/content-systems-miami/` landing pages explicitly.

## 9. Top-Tier Educational Loops
* Routes users upwards into the AEO-optimized content pillars to capture top-of-funnel educational intent.

## 10. Native JSON-LD Injection
* Employs structured `WebSite`, `Organization`, `LocalBusiness`, and `FAQPage` code directly in the DOM head. Answers FAQ queries directly in standard Google-schema form to secure Snippet placement.

## 11. Final Push
* Strong CTA terminating at the `/apply` route.
