# AJ Digital — Blog Article Template (SEO + AEO Optimized)
## Directory: /docs/03-blog/article-template.md
## Purpose: Standardized template for generating high-ranking, AI-citable, conversion-driven articles.

---

# 0. ARTICLE OBJECTIVE

Every article must:

1. Rank on Google (SEO)
2. Be cited by AI systems (AEO)
3. Drive traffic to service pages
4. Convert readers into leads

This is NOT a blog post template.

This is a **traffic + conversion asset template**.

---

# 1. FILE NAMING

/blog/[primary-keyword-hyphenated]/

## Example:
/blog/podcast-production-cost/
/blog/ai-tools-for-entrepreneurs/

---

# 2. HEAD SECTION

<title>[Primary Keyword] | AJ Digital — [Benefit]</title>

<meta name="description" content="[Primary keyword + benefit + CTA]">

<link rel="canonical" href="https://weareajdigital.com/blog/[slug]/">

## OG TAGS:

og:title  
og:description  
og:image → /images/blog/[slug]-og.jpg  

---

# 3. ARTICLE HEADER

Published: [Date]  
Updated: [Date]  
Author: Audio Jones  
Reading time: X min  

---

# 4. H1

# [Primary Keyword — Clear & Direct]

Example:
# Podcast Production Cost (2026 Guide for Business Owners)

---

# 5. INTRO (CRITICAL)

## RULE:
Answer immediately.

## Format (60–80 words):

[Primary keyword] is [direct answer].

[1 sentence explanation]

[1 sentence context or stakes]

[1 sentence preview of article]

---

# 6. TABLE OF CONTENTS

- What is [topic]?
- How much does [topic] cost?
- What affects pricing?
- Best options
- FAQ

---

# 7. SECTION STRUCTURE (REPEAT FOR EACH H2)

## H2:
[User Question]

### AEO ANSWER BLOCK (40–75 words):

Direct, standalone answer.

Must:
- Define the concept
- Include context
- Be quotable by AI

---

### BODY CONTENT:

- 1–3 paragraphs max
- ≤ 3 sentences each
- 35–45 words per paragraph

---

### SUPPORTING ELEMENT (REQUIRED):

Choose ONE:
- Bullet list
- Numbered list
- Table
- Stat

---

# 8. DEFINITION BLOCK RULE

Whenever introducing a concept:

Format:

"[Term] is [definition]. [How it works]. [Why it matters]."

---

# 9. LIST RULES

Each bullet must stand alone.

Example:

- Record with a USB or XLR microphone (minimum quality standard)
- Use Riverside or Descript for remote recording
- Export audio in WAV format for editing

---

# 10. TABLE RULES

Use for:
- comparisons
- tools
- pricing

## Required columns:

| Option | What it does | Price | Best for |

---

# 11. FRAMEWORK INSERTION (HIGH VALUE)

Include at least one named framework.

## Format:

### The AJ Digital [Name] Framework

1. Step
2. Step
3. Step

---

# 12. FAQ SECTION

## H2:
Frequently Asked Questions

### Requirements:
- 6–8 questions
- 40–75 words each
- Direct answers

---

# 13. CTA (MANDATORY)

## Inline CTA:

"Ready to build your [system]? Book a free strategy session."

## Final CTA:

[Book My Free Strategy Session →] → /apply

---

# 14. INTERNAL LINKS (MANDATORY)

Each article must include:

1. Service page link  
2. Related article  
3. CTA (/apply)

---

# 15. SCHEMA (MANDATORY)

Include:

- Article schema
- FAQ schema
- Breadcrumb schema

---

# 16. IMAGE REQUIREMENTS

Each article must include:

- OG image (1200x630)
- 1–3 supporting images

Naming:

/images/blog/[slug]-og.jpg

---

# 17. AEO VALIDATION CHECKLIST

□ H1 includes keyword  
□ Intro answers immediately  
□ Every H2 is a question  
□ Every section has AEO block  
□ Paragraphs are short  
□ List or table included  
□ Framework included  
□ FAQ present  
□ CTA present  
□ Internal links present  
□ Schema included  

---

# 18. GENERATION PROMPT (FOR CLAUDE / GPT)

Use this to generate articles:

---

"Write a 1,500–2,000 word SEO + AEO optimized article using the AJ Digital article template.

Primary keyword: [KEYWORD]

Requirements:
- Answer-first writing
- Question-based H2s
- AEO blocks (40–75 words each)
- Include at least one comparison table
- Include one named framework
- Include 6–8 FAQs
- Include CTA to /apply
- Include internal linking suggestions
- Use clear, direct language
- Avoid fluff or generic intros

Output must follow structured markdown format."

# 20. FINAL STANDARD

An article is successful if:

1. It ranks
2. It gets cited by AI
3. It drives clicks to services
4. It generates leads

If it does not do one of these, it should not exist.
